Privacy Policy

DOUBLI

A playful way to learn · Ages 4-6
Effective: July 2026

Doubli is a screen-time you can feel good about. This page explains what information is collected, how it's used, and the choices you have — in plain language, not legal jargon.

Built for little hands, big smiles, and grown-up peace of mind.

Information We Collect

Doubli stores your child's game progress and star ratings locally on the device. No account, no login, no personal information is required to play.

  • Level progress and star ratings (stored locally only)
  • Sound preference settings
  • No name, email, or any identifying information collected
  • No account creation or registration of any kind

Purchases

🚫📢 No ads anywhere in the app

Doubli is free to download and play. It offers one optional, one-time purchase to unlock additional levels.

  • Handled entirely by Google Play Billing
  • Doubli never sees or stores your payment card details
  • One-time purchase only — no subscriptions, no recurring charges
  • A parent or guardian should always be present for any purchase decision

Data Storage

All game progress is stored locally on the device only. We do not:

  • Transmit personal data to any server (Doubli does not operate one)
  • Sell or share information with third parties
  • Require an account or registration
  • Collect any sensitive personal information

You can clear all locally stored data at any time through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

Doubli integrates with the following third-party service, governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Services (used only to process the optional level-unlock purchase)

Data Safety

💾Game data stored locally on the device only
🚫No ads, no ad tracking, ever
🔒No personal information collected
🗑️Clear all local data anytime via device settings

Children's Privacy

Doubli is designed from the ground up for children ages 4-6, and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

  • No chat, messaging, or user-generated content of any kind
  • No links to outside websites or social media from within gameplay
  • No advertising or ad tracking — Doubli has no ads at all
  • The optional purchase runs entirely through Google Play's own purchase flow, which includes its own parental controls

If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the app following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
