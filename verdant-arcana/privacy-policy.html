Welcome to Verdant Arcana. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

Verdant Arcana stores your reading history and preferences locally on your device using browser or device storage. We do not collect personally identifiable information directly. The following game data is stored locally:

  • Your reading history (which cards were drawn)
  • Your preferences and settings
  • Session data and timestamps
  • No account information or personal data

Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service. AdMob may automatically collect limited technical information, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID)
  • Crash and performance data
  • General usage and interaction information

This information is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

Data Storage

All reading data is stored locally on your device only. We do not:

  • Transmit your personal data to our servers
  • Sell or share your personal information with third parties
  • Require you to create an account or register
  • Collect any sensitive personal information

You can delete all locally stored data at any time through your device or browser settings. Your readings are yours alone.

Third-Party Services

The application may integrate with the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

Data Safety

  • Reading data is stored locally on your device and not shared with us
  • No sensitive personal information is collected by us directly
  • No data encryption is applied to locally stored data (as it remains on your device)
  • You can clear all local data through your device settings at any time
  • Your readings are private to your device

Children's Privacy

Verdant Arcana does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the application following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
