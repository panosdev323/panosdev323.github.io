How to Play

🔍

Find the Anachronism

Scan the scene carefully and tap the modern object that does not belong. A smartphone in Ancient Rome? A rubber duck in a royal bath? Nothing is safe from the vandal of history.

⏱️

Race Against Time

You have 30 seconds. Every wrong tap costs precious time. Find objects quickly to earn Lightning Bonuses and keep your streak alive.

💡

Need a Hint?

Watch a short rewarded ad to freeze the timer and receive a helpful clue. The clock stops while you search.

Learn While Playing

Every discovery reveals fascinating historical facts. Discover secrets from civilizations across time.
